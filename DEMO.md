# Blindspot — 90-Second Demo Script

## Beat-by-Beat Script

**0:00** — Presenter holds up phone with a contract on screen.
"Priya got a freelance offer. ₹4 lakh project. 24 hours to sign or lose the client. She can't afford ₹20,000 for a lawyer. Today, she opens Blindspot."

**0:08** — Upload contract PDF. Select "Freelancer, ₹4L, Bangalore."

**0:12** — Six agent cards appear. Scout activates.
"14 clauses detected. Document type: freelance services agreement."

**0:18** — Investigator card lights up.
"Counterparty: Acme Studios. 2 prior disputes. Risk tier: CAUTION."

**0:25** — Jurist and Benchmarker fill in. Red highlights appear on Clauses 7, 11, 14.

**0:35** — Click Clause 11. Side panel shows all 5 agents' verdicts.

**0:50** — Click "Negotiate". Set parameters. Start negotiation.

**0:58** — Outbound email sent. Negotiation timeline activates.

**1:05** — Inbound email lands. Agents re-evaluate. Auto-respond decision.

**1:25** — Counterparty agrees. Deal closed.

**1:35** — "Priya didn't spend ₹20,000. She spent 90 seconds."

**1:45** — End.
