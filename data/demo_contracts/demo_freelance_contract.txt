FREELANCE SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Freelance Services Agreement ("Agreement") is made on this 1st day of June 2026, by and between:
Acme Studios Pvt Ltd, located at 123 Tech Park, Bangalore ("Client")
AND
Priya Sharma, located at 456 Main St, Mumbai ("Contractor").

1. SERVICES
The Contractor agrees to provide frontend development services for the Client's new web application project.

2. PAYMENT
The Client shall pay the Contractor a fixed fee of ₹5,00,000 for the completion of the services. Payment shall be made within 60 days of receipt of an approved invoice.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
The Contractor hereby assigns to the Client all right, title, and interest in and to any and all work product, inventions, tools, frameworks, and intellectual property, including pre-existing IP and tools developed prior to this engagement, that are used or incorporated into the deliverables.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Contractor agrees to hold all Client information in strict confidence and not disclose it to any third party.

5. NON-COMPETE
The Contractor agrees not to provide similar frontend development services to any competing company in India for a period of 2 years following the termination of this Agreement.

6. TERMINATION
The Client may terminate this Agreement at any time without cause by providing 5 days' written notice to the Contractor. The Contractor may terminate this Agreement by providing 45 days' written notice to the Client.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
